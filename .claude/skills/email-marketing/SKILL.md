# Email Marketing Skill

Write emails that get opened, read, and clicked — with proper structure for deliverability and conversion.

## Workflow

1. **Identify email type** (see types below)
2. **Gather context**: audience segment, goal, offer/CTA, brand voice, ESP (MailerLite/Klaviyo/other)
3. **Write subject line + preview text first** — these determine open rate
4. **Draft email body** using the appropriate template structure
5. **CTA check**: one primary CTA, clear and visible
6. **Deliverability check**: avoid spam triggers, proper text/image ratio guidance

---

## Email Types

### 1. Welcome Email / Onboarding Sequence
- **Goal**: Activate new subscriber, set expectations, deliver lead magnet
- **Timing**: Immediately → Day 3 → Day 7
- **Structure**:
  - Email 1: Welcome + deliver value (lead magnet, resource, or quick win)
  - Email 2: Story / credibility + one helpful tip
  - Email 3: Soft CTA (book a call, explore service, join community)

### 2. Nurture Sequence (B2B)
- **Goal**: Build trust, educate, move toward conversion
- **Timing**: Weekly or bi-weekly
- **Structure**: Problem → Insight → Solution hint → CTA
- **Length**: 200–400 words, plain text or minimal design

### 3. Promotional / Launch Email
- **Goal**: Drive immediate action (purchase, sign-up, book)
- **Structure**:
  - Hook (problem or opportunity)
  - Offer details (what, value, price)
  - Social proof (1–2 lines)
  - Urgency/scarcity
  - CTA button

### 4. Newsletter
- **Goal**: Maintain relationship, provide value, light CTAs
- **Structure**: Intro (1 para) → 2–3 content blocks → Outro + CTA
- **Frequency**: Weekly or bi-weekly max

### 5. Re-engagement Campaign
- **Goal**: Win back inactive subscribers or flag for list cleaning
- **Sequence**: 3 emails over 2 weeks
  - Email 1: "We miss you" + value reminder
  - Email 2: Direct question ("Still interested?")
  - Email 3: "Last chance" + unsubscribe offer

### 6. Transactional / Client Retainer Emails
- **Goal**: Confirm, update, deliver — for agency client communication
- **Tone**: Professional, clear, action-oriented

## Subject Line Formulas

| Formula | Example |
|---|---|
| Curiosity gap | "Why most automations fail (and how to fix yours)" |
| Number + benefit | "5 emails that close B2B deals" |
| Direct offer | "Free audit: is your funnel leaking?" |
| Question | "Are you leaving money on the table?" |
| Social proof | "How [Client] 3x'd their open rates in 30 days" |
| Urgency | "Last 24 hours: free strategy call" |
| Personalization | "[Name], your proposal is ready" |

**Preview text**: Always write 40–90 chars that complement (don't repeat) the subject line.

---

## Copywriting Rules

- **One email, one goal** — never split attention across multiple CTAs
- **Above the fold**: key message visible before scrolling
- **Short paragraphs**: 1–3 sentences max, plenty of whitespace
- **CTA**: Button text = verb + benefit ("Get my free audit", not "Submit")
- **PS line**: Highly read — use for secondary CTA or key reminder
- **Personalization tokens**: Use `{{first_name}}` and segment-specific angles

---

## Deliverability Checklist

- ✅ Sender name is recognizable (person name > brand name)
- ✅ No ALL CAPS in subject line
- ✅ Avoid spam words: free!!!, guarantee, click here, make money
- ✅ Text-to-image ratio: at least 60% text
- ✅ Unsubscribe link present
- ✅ Physical address in footer (legal requirement)
- ✅ Plain text version (auto-generated by most ESPs)

```
## Email: [Name/Number in Sequence]

**Subject line**: [subject]
**Preview text**: [preview]

---

[BODY]

Hi {{first_name}},

[Opening hook — 1–2 sentences]

[Body — problem, insight, or story]

[Transition to CTA]

[CTA Button Text]

[Optional PS]

---

**Notes**:
- Segment: [who receives this]
- Send timing: [when in sequence]
- A/B test: [subject line variant to test]
```

## ESP-Specific Notes

### MailerLite
- Automations: use groups or segments as triggers
- Use "dynamic content" blocks for personalization
- Best for: small-medium lists, simple sequences, newsletters

### Klaviyo
- Flows: event-triggered (Shopify, custom events via API)
- Profiles + properties: leverage for deep segmentation
- Best for: e-commerce, behavioral triggers, revenue attribution
