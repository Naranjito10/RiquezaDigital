# Social Media Calendar Skill

Plan, structure, and write social media content in batches — organized by platform, pillar, and publication week.

## Workflow

1. **Define strategy** (if not already set): audience, platforms, content pillars, posting frequency
2. **Choose production mode**: full monthly calendar / weekly batch / single post / content pillar brainstorm
3. **Write content** using platform-specific formats below
4. **Organize output** in calendar table format
5. **Add scheduling notes** (best times, hashtag strategy, engagement prompts)

---

## Strategy Setup (do once per client)

### Content Pillars (pick 3–5 per brand)
| Pillar | Purpose | % of content |
|---|---|---|
| Education | Teach, add value, build authority | 30–40% |
| Behind the scenes | Build trust, humanize the brand | 15–20% |
| Social proof | Testimonials, results, case studies | 15–20% |
| Promotion | Offers, services, CTAs | 10–15% |
| Engagement | Questions, polls, UGC, trending | 15–20% |

### Platform Selection Guide
| Platform | Best for | Content type |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | B2B, professional services, agency | Long-form posts, carousels, articles |
| Instagram | Visual brands, lifestyle, B2C | Reels, carousels, Stories |
| Facebook | Communities, local business, older demo | Posts, events, groups |
| X/Twitter | Thought leadership, tech, news | Short takes, threads |
| TikTok | Young audience, video-first brands | Short video scripts |

## Platform-Specific Formats

### LinkedIn Post
- **Length**: 150–300 words sweet spot (up to 3,000 chars allowed)
- **Structure**: Hook line (no emoji) → Story or insight → Takeaway → CTA or question
- **Hook rules**: First 2 lines visible before "see more" — must stop the scroll
- **Hashtags**: 3–5 relevant, end of post
- **Best times**: Tue–Thu, 8–10am or 12–1pm

**Hook formulas for LinkedIn**:
- "I made a mistake that cost us €X. Here's what I learned:"
- "Unpopular opinion: [contrarian take]"
- "3 years ago I [action]. Today [result]."
- "Most [professionals] don't know this:"
- "We just [achievement]. Here's exactly how we did it:"

### Instagram Post (Feed)
- **Caption length**: 150 chars for preview; full caption 300–500 words for SEO/reach
- **Structure**: Hook → Story/value → CTA ("save this", "comment X", "link in bio")
- **Hashtags**: 5–10, mix of niche + broad, can go in first comment
- **Carousel**: 7–10 slides; slide 1 = hook, last slide = CTA

### Instagram Reel (script structure)
```
[0–3s] Hook: visual or spoken statement that stops scroll
[3–15s] Setup: context or problem
[15–40s] Payoff: insight, tutorial, or story
[40–60s] CTA: "follow for more", "save this", "comment below"
```

### Facebook Post
- **Length**: 40–80 words for organic reach; longer for groups
- **Images**: Always include — posts with images get 2x engagement
- **CTA**: Ask a direct question to drive comments

| Week | Day | Platform | Pillar | Format | Hook/Title | Copy (or "Draft below") | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| W1 | Mon | LinkedIn | Education | Post | [hook] | [draft] | [hashtags] |
| W1 | Wed | Instagram | Social proof | Carousel | [hook] | [draft] | [slides outline] |
| W1 | Fri | LinkedIn | Behind scenes | Post | [hook] | [draft] | |
| W2 | ... | | | | | | |

**Standard frequencies**:
- LinkedIn: 3x/week (Mon, Wed, Fri)
- Instagram: 4x/week (3 feed + 1 Reel)
- Facebook: 3x/week
- Adjust to client capacity and budget

2. Group by pillar for efficiency (write all education posts together)
3. Repurpose: one long LinkedIn post → Instagram carousel → short tweet thread

## Hashtag Strategy

- **Niche hashtags** (1k–50k posts): highest reach quality
- **Industry hashtags** (50k–500k): medium competition
- **Broad hashtags** (500k+): visibility but buried fast
- Mix: 2 niche + 2 industry + 1 broad per post
